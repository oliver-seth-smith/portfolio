04Market data product Live product

GE Ledger

An Old School RuneScape market dashboard that turns live Wiki/RuneLite price data into tax-adjusted flip rankings, plots high and low price history across five standard ranges, and fits opportunities to a player's cash stack and visible four-hour buy limits.

ReactTypeScriptREST APIsVitestSVGVite
History, calculation and safeguards

Joins mapping, latest-price, and completed 24-hour feeds in parallel, then loads per-item time series on demand for 1D, 1W, 1M, 3M, and 1Y views. Its responsive SVG chart compares average high and low prices, while published four-hour buy limits stay visible on the opportunity board and feed the capital-aware planner. The calculation applies the 2% per-item seller tax—including floor rounding, the 5M gp cap, and dated exemptions—flags stale prices, preserves losses, and avoids presenting market-wide volume as guaranteed profit. Eighty-four automated tests across nine test files cover pricing, API and history handling, caching, filtering, planner math, chart geometry, and accessible UI behavior.

Spotify Stream Analysis

RRegressionKNNData analysis

The regression model outperformed KNN, identifying artist count, Spotify playlist presence, and chart presence as the strongest predictors in the supplied 2023 dataset. The report also documents model assumptions and dataset limitations.
